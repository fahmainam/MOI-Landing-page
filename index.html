Services We Offer

The Ministry of Interior (MOI) constantly improves and simplifies its services delivery channels and procedures. In the light of this, MOI introduces its unique service “Metrash2” that enables Qatari nationals and residents to apply for and obtain more than 224 services that save their time, effort and money, while enjoying the convenience of using their smart phones.

Activating Metrash2 service

Metrash2 Service requires that there must be a mobile number registered by the applicant’s name.

You may obtain and activate Metrash2 Service through SMS.

Activate Metrash2 through SMS

Activate Metrash2 on a Second Phone
Follow these four simple steps

Step 1

Download Metrash2 service from your preferred app store on the second smart phone.

Step 2

Enter your QID number and the password used on the first smart phone.

Step 3

Receive an SMS with a temporary activation key valid for 10 minutes.

Step 4

Enter the activation key and complete the service activation on your second phone.
