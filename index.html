Activate Metrash2 on a Second Phone
Follow these four simple steps

Step 1

Download Metrash2 service from your preferred app store on the second smart phone.

Step 2

Enter your QID number and the password used on the first smart phone.

Step 3

Receive an SMS with a temporary activation key valid for 10 minutes.

Step 4

Enter the activation key and complete the service activation on your second phone.

Need Help with Metrash2?

Find answers to the most common questions related to Metrash2 services provided by the Ministry of Interior – State of Qatar.

Metrash2 is the official mobile application of the Ministry of Interior (MOI) in the State of Qatar. It allows citizens and residents to access more than 200 government services securely through their smartphones.

Services include traffic, residency, visa, citizen services, official requests, notifications, and many other digital government services.

Metrash2 can be used by:

  • Qatari citizens
  • Residents holding a valid QID
  • Companies and sponsors for related services

A valid mobile number registered under the user’s QID is required for activation.

Yes. Metrash2 follows strict security standards implemented by the Ministry of Interior.

  • Secure login with QID and password
  • SMS-based verification and activation codes
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Official government authentication systems

Your personal information is protected and used only for official government services.

  • Vehicle registration and renewal
  • Driving license services
  • Traffic violations and fine payments
  • Vehicle ownership transfer

All traffic services are available 24/7 without visiting traffic departments.

  • Exit permit requests for Qataris
  • Lost document declarations
  • Official document requests
  • Permit cancellation services

Citizen services are processed digitally with instant or fast approval in most cases.
